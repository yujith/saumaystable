Hi, I'm Saumya.
Welcome to my table.

I believe the best way to know someone is to sit at their table. For over thirty years, my kitchen has been the heart of our home—a place where the scent of toasted mustard seeds and fresh curry leaves signals more than just a meal; it signals love.

"That's how Saumya's Table was born."

What started as preparing lunch boxes for my children grew into a passion for sharing the authentic, unhurried flavors of my heritage with our local community. Every dish I prepare is a letter from my kitchen to yours.
