Hi, I'm Saumya.
Welcome to my table.

My journey didn't start in a culinary school, but in a sun-drenched kitchen in Kandy, watching my grandmother transform simple spices into alchemy. There was no recipe book—only the rhythm of the stone grinder and the scent of toasted curry leaves.

After years in the corporate world, I realized that the most meaningful conversations I ever had didn't happen in boardrooms, but around dinner tables. I missed the tactile joy of kneading dough and the slow, unhurried simmer of a clay-pot dhal.

format_quote
"I wanted to create something that felt less like a transaction and more like a letter from a dear friend."

That's how Saumya's Table was born.

Roots & Heritage

Nature’s Rhythm

We source 90% of our produce from local farmers. If it’s not in season, it’s not on the table. We believe in slow growth and zero-waste packaging.
